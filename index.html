Akshay Jamadagni

Dr Akshay Jamadagni is a dentist with special interest in Orthodontics.

If your dental problem is a simple matter, he will be very happy to help. If your problem is more complex, then that is where Akshay excels, in fact he is particularly skillful in helping patients who have difficult problems that other dentists either struggle with or are unable to deal with, and often such patients will be referred to Akshay. For most people, an attractive, healthy smile is very important. If you are not happy with your smile, Akshay would be delighted to help you. He has a special area of interest in making ugly crooked teeth look straight and beautiful!
